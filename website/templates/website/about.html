Our Background

Company Overview

Founded in 2021 and headquartered at Century Executive Tower, Room F12/06, Addis Ababa, East Eagle Trading PLC has rapidly grown into a diversified trading and industrial solutions company serving Ethiopia and the broader East African market.

What began as a focused venture into energy and industrial trading has expanded into a multi-sector enterprise spanning mining, construction machinery, renewable energy, and B2B procurement — anchored by quality, compliance, and reliable service delivery.

We connect international manufacturers and certified suppliers with the region's growing infrastructure, energy, and resource-extraction needs — building long-term partnerships with leading institutions across Ethiopia.
