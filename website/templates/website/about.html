Our Background

Company Overview

East Eagle Trading PLC is an Ethiopian trading company established in 2021, engaged in import, export, and international business activities. Headquartered at Century Executive Tower, Room F12/06, Addis Ababa, the company is committed to delivering quality products and services while building strong partnerships with customers, suppliers, and stakeholders.

Leveraging Ethiopia's strategic position in regional and global trade, East Eagle facilitates the movement of goods and commodities across borders — contributing to economic growth, job creation, and sustainable business development.

Since founding, the company has expanded across mining exports, construction machinery, renewable energy, FMCG, and computer distribution — while maintaining a disciplined commitment to quality, compliance, and reliable service delivery.
