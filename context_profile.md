## Company Establishment

**East Eagle Trading PLC** was **established in 2021** in **Addis Ababa, Ethiopia**.

Since its founding, the company has grown from a focused trading venture into a diversified import–export enterprise serving Ethiopia and the broader East African market. In a few short years, East Eagle has expanded across mining exports, construction machinery, renewable energy, FMCG, and computer distribution — while maintaining a disciplined commitment to quality, compliance, and reliable service delivery.

## Company Overview

East Eagle Trading PLC is an Ethiopian trading company **established in 2021**, engaged in **import, export, and international business activities**. The company is committed to delivering quality products and services while building strong partnerships with customers, suppliers, and stakeholders across local and international markets.

Leveraging Ethiopia's strategic position in regional and global trade, East Eagle Trading PLC facilitates the movement of goods and commodities across borders, contributing to economic growth, job creation, and sustainable business development.
